What we do

We are a gym that caters specificially to stay-at-home mums. With everything they need under one-roof, it will bring peace of mind knowing that their little ones are within the same building and well taken off by our professional nannies, thus making their time in our gym an enjoyable one.

Why we do what we do

The life of a stay-home-mum is often busy. Sometimes, she forgets about her own needs. A balanced of mind and body is good vital for anyone's overall well-being and happiness. That's where we come in. We want her to know that while she's taking care of everyone else's needs, we are taking care of hers.
